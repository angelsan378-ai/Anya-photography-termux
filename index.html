About

Toronto & GTA · Portrait · Street · Pets

Capturing people and moments
with honesty, warmth,
and emotion.

Anya Stsetsevich is a portrait, street, and pet photographer based in Toronto and the GTA. Her work is centered around the beauty of natural expression — the unfiltered laughter, quiet vulnerability, spontaneous movement, and small details that make every person and every story unique.

Rather than creating perfect images, Anya focuses on preserving real feelings and genuine connection exactly as they are. Inspired by everyday life and human stories, she creates photographs that feel alive, personal, and timeless — images that hold emotion long after the moment has passed.

"Here will be a phrase."

Every session is a collaboration — a space where people can be themselves, feel comfortable, and let their true personality shine through. Whether it's a family gathering, a quiet portrait, a playful pet, or a fleeting street moment, the goal is always the same: to tell your story with authenticity and care.
